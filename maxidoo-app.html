About project

Maxidoo is a Cloud based solution enabling hotels automate their operation & boost revenue.

Maxidoo Hotel Management Software is one of the best HMS software that can efficiently coordinate activities such as supervising the subordinates, hiring and training, performance evaluation of employees, preparing the budget for front office department, and more. Being a hotel billing software, Maxidoo Hotel Management Software allows multi-user access so that the authorised persons can access it from different locations to remain in sync with the daily business happenings.

This hotel management software allows you to send Email or SMS to customers to nurture the potential business clientele and grow your business exponentially.

The Challenges

Maxidoo already thrived as a web application, but there was a growing demand for mobile accessibility, especially among hotel staff who might not be so good in term of technology. The challenges were clear

User-centred Approach : Design a user interface that caters to hotel owners, managers, and staff, ensuring ease of use for all, regardless of technical expertise.

Maintaining Brand Consistency : Replicate the look and feel of the existing web app while optimizing it for the mobile environment.

Audience Personas :

Hotel Owner (Persona: John) : A middle-aged hotel owner who values efficiency. John is not particularly tech-savvy and needs an intuitive solution to manage his hotel operations effectively.

Hotel Manager (Persona: Sarah) : Sarah, the hotel manager, is tech-savvy and values a platform that streamlines tasks, enabling her to focus on guest experience.

Hotel Staff (Persona: David) : David, a frontline staff member, isn't very comfortable with technology. The app must be user-friendly, allowing him to perform daily tasks effortlessly.
